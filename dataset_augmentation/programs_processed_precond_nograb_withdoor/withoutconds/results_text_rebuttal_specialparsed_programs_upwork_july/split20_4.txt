Brush hair
Stand in front of mirror.  Pick up hair brush, run brush through hair until hair is neat.  Put brush back down.


[Walk] <BATHROOM> (1)
[Find] <HAIRBRUSH> (1)
[Grab] <HAIRBRUSH> (1)
[Find] <MIRROR> (1)
[LookAt] <MIRROR> (1)
[Find] <HAIR> (1)
[Brush] <HAIR> (1)
[PutObjBack] <HAIRBRUSH> (1)
